    Problem And Motivation

    Why this is a blockchain systems problem, not only a trading idea

    While finding statistical alpha is difficult, pooling capital to execute it introduces a major trust boundary. The core infrastructure challenge is ensuring that off-chain trading states and NAV updates remain cryptographically transparent to on-chain depositors without running heavy analytics directly on the EVM.
